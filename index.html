CONTENT CLASSIFICATION CODES

Identify the exact defect. Choose from five possible report codes:

Safe
Legitimate / Verified Claim

Factually realistic, includes proper caveats, standard metrics, and refers to known authentic bodies. No logical fallacies detected.

Fake Stat
Mathematical Hallucinations

Overly precise decimals (e.g. 74.83910%), values over 100% when describing shares, negative metrics where they don't apply, or math that doesn't sum up.

Fake Quote
Anachronisms & Misattributions

Famous people talking about technologies that didn't exist in their era (e.g., Julius Caesar talking about relational database indexes, Steve Jobs on TikTok).

Fake Source
URL Scepticism & Fake Institutions

Lookalike domains (e.g. `paypal.com-login-update.xyz`, `nasa.gov.leak-files.net.ru`) or invented departments like the 'Harvard Institute of Absolute Certainty'.

Misleading Claim
Exaggerations & Correlation Errors

Absolute guarantees (100% bug-free for life), absurd multipliers (4,000% speed increases instantly), and false cause-and-effect claims.
